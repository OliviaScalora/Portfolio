Predictive Modeling and Data Analysis

Predicting Employee Turnover | Guilford County, North Carolina

A proof-of concept analytical and informational system which will associate a voluntary turnover risk score with each employee such that appropriate preventative measures can be taken by Human Resources to increase employee retention. Developed for Guilford County, North Carolina Human Resources.

Predicting Opioid Overdoses | Mesa, Arizona

A geospatial risk model to predict the liklihood of opioid overdose occurences in Mesa, Az, a city in the midst of a crisis. The project highlights locations in Mesa where intervention is recommended based on the predictions. A hypothetical web application is conceptualized to demonstrate the applicability of the model.

Locating Eviction Risk | Philadelphia, PA

A dashboard which illustrates eviction risk through L&I violations and complaints as well as expiring housing subsidies. An awareness and advocacy tool for policy makers to understand Philadelphia neighborhoods in greatest need of limited resources.

Predicting House Sale Price | Boulder City, CO

Proximity to natural geographic features such as lakes and mountains, municipal boundaries, demographic data, and building features are used to build a predictive model for future house sale prices in Boulder City, CO based on observed past sale prices.

Memo to the Mayor | Reducing Recidivism Through Fair Algorithms

An exercise in addressing baked-in biases of data through cost/benefit analysis.

Renter's Rights and Landlord Transparency

Research writing sample published in the 2021 edition of UPenn's Planning student journal, 'Panorama'. This essay breaks down the current practices of renters rights' and landlord transparency in Philadelphia.
