Predictive Modeling and Data Analysis

Predicting Opioid Overdoses | Mesa, Arizona

A geospatial risk model was built to predict the liklihood of opioid overdose occurences in Mesa, Az, a city in the midst of a crisis. The project highlights locations in Mesa where intervention is recommended based on the predictions. A hypothetical web application is conceptualized to demonstrate the applicability of the model.

Locating Eviction Risk | Philadelphia, PA

The need for safe, well-maintained, and affordable housing is an urgent need for many in Philadelphia. The goal of our dashboard is not only to bring additional awareness to the impact the intersection of these two expirations on the Philadelphia community, but also to explore whether there are neighborhoods who may experience the evictions and subsidy expirations more than others and, thus, need solutions sooner. This information is critical for policy makers seeking to aim limited resources in the most effective way.
